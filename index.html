About Us

Welcome To Gothenburg!


A city of rich culture, beautiful architecture and perfect to discover by bicycle! Join our knowledgable, local guides as they whisk you away through the numerous sites Gothenburg has to offer. Starting from our meet-up point in the city, we then make our way down towards the coast before we stop and enjoy lunch by the water.
